Recipes

Recipes that are just as tasty as they are healthy. With little effort, you can make these recipes and they are easy on your pocket.

Easy Avocado Toast

Make this simple speedy breakfast with just a handful of ingredients. Our avocado toast uses crusty sourdough bread and a pinch of chilli for a kick

Healthy Chicken Salad

Try this no-fuss, midweek meal that's high in protein and big on flavour. Marinate chicken breasts, then drizzle with a punchy peanut satay sauce

Quick Stir Fry

Crunchy veg, smooth noodles and soft tofu, combined with a sweet and spicy sauce. This healthy vegetarian dinner for two is ready in just 30 minutes
