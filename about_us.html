About Us

Going to uni and never cooked before? This website is for you!

Balancing work with studies leaves us with little time to cook. And when it's time to eat, the takeaway menu seems so appealing. But this may not always be the healthiest option.

If you don't want to compromise on taste and nutrition while saving some money, we've created some quick and easy recipes that are perfect for college students...or anyone with a busy lifestyle. These recipes are made and approved by college students so you can rest assured that you're time and money will be saved.

We've also made curated guides to grocery shopping, meal prepping and making use of your food scraps so that nothing goes to waste. It's better for the planet, and it's better for you!
